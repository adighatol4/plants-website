Some common questions

How long does it take for oranges to grow on a tree after planting?

Oranges typically take about 3 to year 4 to grow and ripen on a tree after planting, depending on the variety and growing conditions.

How often should I water my orange/lemon tree?

Water your orange/lemon tree deeply once a week, allowing the soil to dry out between waterings.

Which types of orange plants are available in your nursery?

Nagpuri Santra , Rangpuri Santra , Katol Gold Santra ,

What are some easy ways to fertilize my plants?

You can use slow-release fertilizers, liquid fertilizers, or organic compost to provide nutrients to your plants.

When is the best time to plant an orange tree

The best time to plant an orange tree in a region with a rainy season is typically at the beginning of the rainy season. This timing allows the tree to take advantage of the increased moisture in the soil, which can help it establish strong roots.
